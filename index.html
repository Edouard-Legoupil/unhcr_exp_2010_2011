Visualization of Growing Financial & Human Resources Commitment to Emergencies between 2010 and 2011

Since 2010 UNHCR have had to face a conjunction of severe humanitarian emergencies.

This map shows how financial resources, field offices number, emergency response teams deployment and asylum seekers outflows evolved between 2010 and 2011.
